# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We want Obsidian Linux to be a respectful, technically honest, and welcoming open-source project. Contributors are expected to communicate constructively, assume good intent, and avoid harassment or dismissive behavior.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful in technical disagreement.
- Focus criticism on ideas and implementation, not people.
- Be honest about verification status and limitations.
- Help keep the project accessible to new contributors.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or discrimination
- Personal attacks
- Deliberate misinformation about project status
- Publishing private or sensitive information without consent
